Test guide · Trial week

Welcome to your new system, Helle

This guide shows you what to try in the test version, what works, and what isn't ready yet. Everything here is safe to click — no real messages are sent to real people.

Note for Gergely: this is the English review copy. The Danish version Helle receives is at /dov-test-guide-7m2kx9/. App button/menu names are kept in Danish (in quotes) since that's what she'll see on screen.

The most important things to know first

  • This is a test version of the system — not the real, public site yet.
  • Every email and SMS is marked “[TEST]” and goes only to you and Gergely. No real vikars or stores are ever contacted, no matter what you do. Explore freely.
  • The system starts completely empty (no bookings, no users). You build up test data yourself — just like a normal working day. Everything is cleared again before the real launch.
  • You log in as the administrator — the person with the full overview.

1Getting started

1Open the invitation

Find the email with the subject “[TEST] Invitation til Den Optiske Vikar” in your inbox (helle@denoptiskevikar.dk). Click the link in the email.

2Choose your password

On the page that opens, choose a password (at least 8 characters) and confirm it. Click “Aktiver konto” (Activate account). Your administrator account is now created.

3Log in

You'll be taken to the login page. Log in with your email and the password you just chose. You'll land on the administrator overview. From now on you log in at the same place: den-optiske-vikar-staging-c6udm.ondigitalocean.app/login

Forgot your password? Click “Glemt adgangskode?” (Forgot password?) on the login page — you'll get another email (also marked [TEST]) with a link to choose a new one.

2What to test — step by step

The best test is to use the system the way you would on a real day. Here is a recommended order. You don't have to do it all at once.

Tip for testing on your own: Because we're in test mode, all invitation emails come to your inbox. So you can invite a “test vikar” or a “test store” using any email address (e.g. test-vikar@denoptiskevikar.dk), open the invitation from your own inbox, and set it up yourself in a private/incognito window — that lets you see how the vikar and the store experience the system.

ALook around as administrator

Explore the top menu: “Oversigt” (Overview), “Bookinger” (Bookings), “Eskaleringer” (Escalations), “Kunder” (Customers), and the “Agenter” and “Mere” (Agents / More) drop-downs. Notice the daily briefing on the front page. Everything is empty to start with — that's intended.

BInvite a vikar

Go to “Administrer → Vikarer” (Manage → Vikars) and click “Inviter vikar” (Invite vikar). Fill in a name and email, and leave Rolle (Role) set to “Vikar”. Send. Open the invitation email (it comes to you), choose a password via the link, and log in as the vikar in an incognito window. See the vikar portal: “Mine Bookinger”, “Kalender” (with availability), “Mine Afregninger”, and “Min Profil” (My Bookings / Calendar / My Settlements / My Profile).

CCreate a store (customer) and invite it

Go to “Kunder” and create a new contact/store. Send a portal invitation to the store, and set it up the same way (open the email → choose a password → log in in another incognito window). See the customer portal: “Oversigt”, “Bookinger”, “Kreditter”, “Timer”, “Anmodninger”, and “Profil” (Overview / Bookings / Credits / Hours / Requests / Profile).

DMake a booking and respond to it

As the store: click “Book en vikar” (Book a vikar), go through the steps (reason, days, hours, optional preferred vikar, location) and send. As the vikar: open the request and try the three responses — “Accepter”, “Accepter med vilkår”, and “Afvis” (Accept / Accept with condition / Decline). Notice that the store and the vikar each get their own message along the way (to your inbox).

ETry the “accept with condition” flow

Have the vikar accept with a condition (e.g. “can't start until 10:00”). Switch to the store — the condition is shown, and the store can “Godkend” (Approve) or “Afvis” (Reject) it. If approved, the booking is confirmed. If rejected, the booking reopens (for renegotiation) and the vikar is notified.

FTry cancellation with approval

As the store: ask to cancel a confirmed booking (one that has a vikar assigned). Notice it becomes a request sent to you as the administrator. Switch to the administrator, go to “Eskaleringer”, and approve the cancellation. The booking is cancelled and the vikar is notified. (A booking with no vikar assigned can be cancelled by the store immediately.)

GAssign a vikar as administrator

Create a booking with no preferred vikar (an “open” request). Go in as the administrator and assign a vikar to it. The vikar is notified (email + SMS to your inbox/phone).

HCheck that the messages arrive

Keep an eye on your inbox and phone throughout. At each important step (new request, confirmation, condition, cancellation) an email and/or SMS should arrive — marked [TEST]. Both you and Gergely receive all of them, so you can both follow what's being tested.

5How to report back

You don't need a system for it — a simple list is enough. For each thing you notice, please write:

  • What did you do? (e.g. “created a booking as a store”)
  • What did you expect?
  • What happened instead? (a screenshot helps)

Send your list to Gergely. Because all messages also come to him, he can see most of what you test — but your own notes about what felt wrong or awkward are worth their weight in gold.

6Glossary

AdministratorYou, Helle — you have the full overview and manage vikars, stores and bookings.
VikarThe optician who steps in at a store. In the system the role is called “vikar”.
Store / Customer (Butik / Kunde)The optician business that orders a vikar.
BookingA request for a vikar to cover a shift for one or more days.
Condition (Vilkår)A condition a vikar can set when accepting (e.g. “can't start until 10:00”), which the store then approves or rejects.
Escalation (Eskalering)A case sent to you as administrator to decide on — e.g. a cancellation request.
Credits (Kreditter)Prepaid vikar hours (payment — coming in a later phase).
[TEST]The marker on all messages in the test environment. It means the message only went to you + Gergely, not to real recipients.
Incognito windowA private browser window (Ctrl+Shift+N in Chrome) where you can be logged in as a different user at the same time — useful for testing the vikar and store side.
