Automated HNW Investor Lead Pipeline for Hedge Fund

Create an AI Agent to Find Investors for a US Based Hedge Fund

In short

Gergely proposes an end-to-end AI agent that scrapes high-signal sources (LinkedIn, SEC EDGAR Form D, alt-investment forums, conference lists), scores each lead against your three criteria using Claude, and delivers enriched, confidence-scored profiles to a CRM on a schedule. Wealth at $5M+ is proxy-scored from public signals — LP/GP filings, real estate records, title and tenure — and flagged as probabilistic until you verify in conversation. MVP pipeline ships in 1–2 weeks; full deployment with outreach integration in 3–4 weeks.

Architecture I'd propose

  • Apify Scraper — LinkedIn, EDGAR Form D, forums, event lists
  • Claude Scorer — 3-criteria rubric: location, interest, wealth signals
  • Wealth Proxy — LP/GP filings, real estate, title + tenure
  • Human Review Queue — Borderline leads flagged before contact
  • Airtable / CRM — Enriched profile + confidence score
  • Instantly (optional) — Sequenced outreach on approval
Assumes: Assumes Airtable as the CRM output — Google Sheets or HubSpot are a straight swap · Assumes Instantly for outreach sequencing if that module is in scope · Assumes LinkedIn scraping via Apify within platform rate limits — tell me where I'm wrong and the design adapts.

Plan

  • Phase 1 - MVP Pipeline (Week 1–2) — Scrape → score → enriched list you can run immediately
  • Phase 2 - Scheduled Agent (Week 3) — CRM delivery on a recurring schedule
  • Phase 3 - Outreach Integration (Week 4) — Instantly sequencing, full end-to-end automation
