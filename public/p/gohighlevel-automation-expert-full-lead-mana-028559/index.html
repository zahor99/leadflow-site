GoHighLevel Automation Expert — Full Lead Management System

In short

A complete automated lead management system inside your GHL sub-account — instant response, AI SMS qualification, multi-stage nurture, and hot-lead escalation, built snapshot-ready for reuse across future clients. Every stage from new lead to won/lost is wired end to end, with no manual data entry in the flow.

Architecture I'd propose

  • Lead Entry — Meta form / call / DM webhook
  • Instant Response — GHL workflow fires in seconds
  • AI SMS Bot — Qualifies, asks, books or escalates
  • Hot-Lead Alert — Intent trigger → Slack/SMS to rep
  • Nurture Sequences — SMS + email, intent-driven branching
  • Pipeline Stages — 9 stages, new lead → won/lost
Assumes: Assumes Meta lead forms connect via GHL native webhook or Zapier · Assumes calendar is GHL-native and attached to the sub-account · Assumes Slack is available for hot-lead rep notifications · Assumes SMS sending complies with existing opt-in capture — tell me where I'm wrong and the design adapts.

Plan

  • Phase 1 — Pipeline + Triggers (Week 1) — Live pipeline, instant-response flows, Meta webhook wired
  • Phase 2 — AI Bot Logic (Week 2) — Qualify → book or escalate, hot-lead alerts active
  • Phase 3 — Nurture + Snapshot (Weeks 3–4) — Nurture sequences, edge-case testing, snapshot packaged
